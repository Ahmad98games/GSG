# Rice Mill Management Software Pakistan

Rice milling is one of Pakistan's largest
agricultural industries. Punjab alone has
over 2,000 registered rice mills.

Yet most rice mills track their operations

## The Challenges of Rice Mill Management

**Paddy procurement tracking:**
Multiple farmers, different varieties,
different prices. Manual tracking loses money.

**Milling yield calculation:**
How much processed rice comes from
100 maunds of paddy? If the yield is lower
than expected, where did the difference go?

**Dealer payments:**
Dealers often buy on credit. Tracking who
owes what across dozens of dealers manually
leads to disputes and losses.
