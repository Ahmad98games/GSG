# How AI CCTV Prevents Factory Inventory Theft

Most manufacturing facilities in Pakistan have standard security cameras installed. Whether it is a textile unit in Faisalabad or a light-engineering workshop in Lahore, you will see cameras mounted on walls.

But here is the sad truth: most of these cameras are completely passive. 

They only record footage to a local DVR. When raw material or expensive spare parts go missing, the owner or security officer has to spend hours scrubbing through blurry footage to find out what happened. By then, the material is gone, and the damage is done.

## The Problem with Passive Monitoring

**1. No immediate alerts**
Traditional CCTV systems do not know the difference between a cat walking by and a person entering the warehouse after midnight.

**2. Fatigue**
It is physically impossible for a security guard to watch 16 screens continuously for an 8-hour shift. Studies show guards miss over 90% of suspicious activity after just 20 minutes of staring at monitors.

**3. Poor evidence**
By the time you notice something is stolen (often days later), finding the exact segment of footage is incredibly difficult.
