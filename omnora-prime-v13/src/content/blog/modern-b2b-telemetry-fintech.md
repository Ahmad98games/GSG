# Modern Tools for Factory Floors: How Noxis Hub is Helping Local Workshops

Manufacturing workshops and textile mills in South Asia have long operated in a technological blind spot. Without specialized software, factory owners relied on scattered Excel spreadsheets or physical registers to calculate wages for dozens of **Karigars** (piece-rate workers), track inventory volumes, and log payments. These older methods inevitably led to arguments, missing stock, and cash flow problems.

With the release of **Noxis Hub v13**, we are introducing a unified set of easy-to-use factory tools designed to make factory floor tracking simple, automated, and connected to your accounts. Here is a look at the five core systems now running live.

---

## 1. Local Market Price Insights

Operating across major industrial hubs including Pakistan, UAE, Turkey, and Bangladesh, the **Noxis Smart Market Tool** gets live updates on market prices.

By analyzing average wages and raw material costs in local markets, the software gives factory owners accurate insights into competitive rates. Instead of guessing piece rates for new fabric, owners are backed by clear data reflecting active wage averages in their specific area (such as Faisalabad weaving, Lahore textile processing, or Dubai manufacturing).

---

## 2. On-Device Smart Stock Alerts

Cloud reliance is a major liability on factory floors where electricity grids and internet coverage are notoriously unstable. To resolve this, Noxis v13 implements smart on-device alerts.

Operating entirely locally on office computers without needing internet, the **Smart Alerts Engine** tracks the speed of your raw material usage. It calculates when your stock is running low and gives you alerts up to 4 days in advance, so you have plenty of time to order more. Furthermore, it studies customer purchase trends to warn you if a regular client is placing fewer orders, protecting your business profits.

---

## 3. Simple Business Credit & Loan Scoring

Access to bank loans is one of the steepest challenges faced by small and medium-sized factories. Noxis bridges this gap by turning your daily transaction logs into proof of business health.

The **Working Capital Portal** implements a simple scoring system. Based on four parameters—ledger transaction history, monthly revenue, profit margin stability, and Peshgi (salary advance) ratios—the system calculates a secure **Business Trust Rating (Grades A-D)**. Factories scoring in the top brackets are instantly connected to bank partners and payout networks (like JazzCash or EasyPaisa) to secure business loans.

---

## 4. Secure Connections (APIs)

To facilitate business growth, Noxis Hub is introducing an open connection gateway. Developer accounts can now create secure connection keys and receive instant transaction updates.

Every transaction, ledger update, or stock change can be sent to third-party accounts software or custom reporting setups instantly. Using secure verification keys, the gateway guarantees that data sync remains highly secure, fast, and protected.

---

## 5. Verified Worker Digital Identities

The backbone of any factory is its floor staff. By introducing **Verified Worker Profiles**, Noxis is empowering Karigars with a portable digital work card.

Every staff profile registers active skills, supervisor-reviewed performance stats, verified work history, and attendance records. Deployed as public QR-code web pages, these cards allow workers to demonstrate their credentials securely to overseas employers, securing premium job offers while removing verification delays for factories.

---

## The Path Forward

The digitalization of manufacturing is no longer a cosmetic upgrade—it is a competitive necessity. By unifying live activity reports, smart on-device alerts, loan scoring portals, secure connections, and staff registries, Noxis Hub v13 is setting the standard for the modern workshop.
