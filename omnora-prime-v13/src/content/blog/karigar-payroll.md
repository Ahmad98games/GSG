# How to Manage Karigar Payroll Without Excel

Every textile factory in Pakistan has the same problem. Dozens of Karigars. Each with different rates. Some on piece rate. Some on daily wages. Some on monthly salary.

At the end of the month, the owner or manager sits with a stack of papers and a calculator. Hours later, wages are calculated. Mistakes happen. Arguments happen.

## The Excel Problem

Most factories use Excel for Karigar management. The problems with Excel:

**1. No attendance tracking**
Excel cannot track who came on which day. You write it manually and hope you remembered.

**2. Piece rate calculation errors**
Grade A pieces at one rate, Grade B at another, rejected at zero. One formula error and everyone gets wrong wages.

**3. Peshgi not tracked properly**
When a Karigar takes an advance, it should be deducted from wages automatically. In Excel, this is manual every time.

**4. No audit trail**
If a dispute happens: who changed this number? When was it changed? Excel cannot tell you.
