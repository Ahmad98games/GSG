# License Activation

**Persona**: Business Owner / Administrator

Activate or renew your Noxis Hub industrial license.

### Prerequisites
- 24-digit License Key provided by Omnora Labs.
- Temporary internet connection for activation (offline activation available for Elite).

### Step-by-Step Instructions
1. Open **Settings > License Management**.
2. Click **Activate New License**.
3. Enter your **License Key** exactly as it appears in your purchase email.
4. Click **Submit for Verification**.
5. Once activated, the Hub will unlock the features corresponding to your tier (Lite/Pro/Elite).

### Troubleshooting
- **Invalid Key**: Ensure you are not confusing similar characters (e.g., '0' and 'O', '1' and 'I').
- **Already in Use**: Standard licenses are limited to one workstation. Contact support for a **Device Re-binding** request.
