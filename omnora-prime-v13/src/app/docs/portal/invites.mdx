# Inviting Clients

**Persona**: Sales Manager / Accounts Manager

Grant clients access to their own transaction history and ledger statements.

### Prerequisites
- Active internet connection for the initial invite dispatch.
- Client record must have a valid email address.

### Step-by-Step Instructions
1. Navigate to the **Client Portal** module.
2. Search for the **Party (Client)** you wish to invite.
3. Click the **Send Portal Invite** button.
4. The client will receive an email with a secure, one-time-use link.
5. Once they register, you can monitor their **Last Login** status from the dashboard.

### Troubleshooting
- **Email Not Received**: Check the client's spam folder or verify the email address in the **Parties** module.
- **Link Expired**: Invites are valid for 24 hours. Use the **Re-invite** button to generate a new link.
