# Adding Cameras

**Persona**: Security Manager / System Admin

Integrate RTSP-capable CCTV cameras into the Noxis Sentinel monitoring system.

### Prerequisites
- IP Camera supporting the **RTSP** protocol.
- Camera IP address, username, and password.

### Step-by-Step Instructions
1. Navigate to **Sentinel CCTV > Manage Nodes**.
2. Click **Register New Node**.
3. Enter the **Node Label** (e.g., Gate 1, Loading Bay).
4. Provide the **RTSP URL** (e.g., `rtsp://admin:pass@192.168.1.50:554/stream1`).
5. Set the **Detection Sensitivity** for AI motion tracking.
6. Click **Test Connection** to verify the video feed.
7. Click **Activate Node**.

### Troubleshooting
- **No Video Feed**: Ensure the camera is pingable from the Hub workstation.
- **Protocol Mismatch**: Verify that the RTSP port (default 554) is open on your network firewall.
