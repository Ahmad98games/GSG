# Barcode Scanning

**Persona**: Warehouse Operator

Execute stock movements and shipments using industrial barcode scanners.

### Prerequisites
- Compatible HID-compliant barcode scanner connected to the workstation.
- SKU labels printed with generated barcodes.

### Step-by-Step Instructions
1. Open the **Scanning Interface** from the dashboard.
2. Select the operation type: **Inward (Purchase)** or **Outward (Sales)**.
3. Position the scanner over the SKU barcode.
4. The system will automatically fetch the product details and record the movement.
5. Enter the quantity received or dispatched.
6. Press **Confirm Transaction**.

### Troubleshooting
- **Scanner Not Responding**: Ensure the scanner is in "Keyboard Emulation" mode.
- **Unrecognized Barcode**: Verify the SKU exists in the database.
- **Wrong Quantity**: Use the **Audit Log** to correct entry errors manually.
