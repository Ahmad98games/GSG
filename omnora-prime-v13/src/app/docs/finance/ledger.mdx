# Understanding the Ledger

**Persona**: Business Owner / Manager

Monitor the financial health of your industrial operation through real-time Khata tracking.

### Prerequisites
- Basic understanding of double-entry accounting.

### Step-by-Step Instructions
1. Navigate to **Finance > Central Ledger**.
2. Filter the view by **Date Range** or **Account Head**.
3. Observe the **Debit** and **Credit** columns to track value movement.
4. Click on any entry to see the **Source Document** (Invoice/Voucher).
5. Export the **Trial Balance** for external audit purposes.

### Troubleshooting
- **Imbalance**: If the ledger does not balance, check for manually edited database entries or interrupted sync processes.
- **Missing Entries**: Verify that the corresponding module (Stock/Payroll) has "Auto-post to Ledger" enabled in settings.
