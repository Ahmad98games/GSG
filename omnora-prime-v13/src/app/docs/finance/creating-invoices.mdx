# Financial Invoicing Workflow

Invoices in Noxis Hub are integrated with the double-entry ledger, ensuring every sale is balanced against cash or accounts receivable.

## Invoice Types
- **Cash Invoice**: Immediate payment settled to the Cash/Bank account.
- **Credit Invoice**: Posted to the Customer's Khata (ledger).
- **Proforma**: Temporary invoice for quotation purposes (does not affect ledger).

## Creating an Invoice

1. **Select Counterparty**
   Search for an existing customer or create a new "Walk-in" party.

2. **Add Line Items**
   Scan barcodes or search for SKUs. Adjust quantities and manual discounts if authorized.
   [DIAGRAM]: Select Item -> Apply Discount -> Calculate Tax -> Finalize Total

3. **Choose Payment Mode**
   Select Cash, Card, Bank Transfer, or JazzCash. For credit sales, select **"Post to Khata"**.

4. **Verify Tax Calculations**
   Ensure the correct GST/VAT category is applied. Noxis Hub automatically calculates the tax based on your regional settings.

5. **Finalize and Print**
   Click **"Post Invoice"**. This will:
   - Deduct stock from Inventory.
   - Credit the Sales Account.
   - Debit the Cash/Bank or Accounts Receivable.

## Reversing an Invoice (Returns)
To return an item, use the **Sales Return** module. Never delete a posted invoice; instead, issue a Credit Note to maintain the audit trail required for industrial compliance.
